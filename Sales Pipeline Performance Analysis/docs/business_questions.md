# Project Objectives

The objective of this project is to evaluate the company's sales pipeline and identify opportunities to improve revenue generation, sales efficiency, and forecasting accuracy.

This analysis focuses on **six key business questions**:
### 1. Where are we losing opportunities in the sales pipeline?

- **Business value**: Identifying the stages with the highest drop-off can help improve conversion rates and prioritize process improvements.
### 2. Which sales representatives consistently generate the strongest results?

- **Business value**: Understanding top-performing behaviors can inform coaching, territory planning, and performance management.


### 3. Which products generate the most revenue and have the highest win rates?

- **Business value**: Helps leadership prioritize product strategy and sales focus.


### 4. Which industries represent the strongest sales opportunities?

- **Business value**: Identifies the customer segments with the highest revenue potential and conversion rates.


### 5. How long does it typically take to close a deal?

- **Business value**: Provides insight into the sales cycle and helps improve forecasting accuracy.


### 6. Which regional offices or managers are outperforming others?

- **Business value**: Highlights operational differences that can be replicated across teams.
